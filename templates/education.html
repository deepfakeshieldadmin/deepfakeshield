How Authenticity Detection Works

DeepFake Shield combines metadata, visual analysis, audio features, and text heuristics to estimate media authenticity.

EXIF Metadata

Camera images often contain EXIF data such as device make, model, and capture settings. Missing EXIF can indicate screenshots, edited images, or AI-generated content.

Artifacts

Synthetic images can contain visual artifacts such as abnormal frequency distribution, unnatural smoothness, or compression inconsistencies.

Audio Features

Audio is checked using RMS energy, spectral flatness, clipping ratio, harmonic structure, and other signal-level characteristics.

Text Heuristics

AI text often shows unusual repetition, low burstiness, predictable punctuation patterns, and lower sentence diversity than natural writing.

Score Interpretation

  • 0–39 Likely Fake / AI Generated
  • 40–74 Suspicious / Possibly Edited
  • 75–99 Likely Real
  • 100 Highly Authentic
