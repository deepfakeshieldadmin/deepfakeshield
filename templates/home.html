Understanding Authenticity

What is EXIF?

EXIF stores camera make, model, date, GPS, and technical settings. Its presence often indicates an original camera capture.

Missing EXIF

Screenshots, edited images, and AI-generated content typically lack EXIF metadata, reducing authenticity confidence.

Social Media Effect

Platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp strip metadata during upload. This is why score alone is not final proof.

Edited Images

Photoshop and editing tools can modify metadata. Our engine checks for inconsistencies beyond metadata alone.
