Privacy Policy

How we handle your data and protect your privacy

Data Collection

DeepFake Shield collects the following information:

  • Account information (username, email address)
  • Uploaded media files for analysis purposes
  • Analysis results and scan history
  • Session data for authentication

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and analyzing uploaded media files
  • Maintaining your scan history and dashboard
  • Generating analysis reports
  • Account authentication and security

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Hashed password storage using Django's secure hashing
  • CSRF protection on all forms
  • Session-based authentication with secure cookies

Data Retention

Uploaded media files and analysis results are stored securely and associated with your account. You may request deletion of your data by contacting the system administrator.

Cookies

We use essential cookies only for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • CSRF token protection
  • Theme preference storage

No third-party tracking cookies are used.

Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact the system administrator through the admin panel or project team.
